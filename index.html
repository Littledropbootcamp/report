2022

2022 has been an amazing year for us at Little drop Education Bootcamp, from onboarding and reaching locations outside Nigeria, to mentees completing their courses, to mentees getting employed, We look forward to another year of growth at Little Drop Bootcamp.

What We Stand For

Little Drop Education Coding Bootcamp is an online-based not-for-profit charity where our mentees with little or no coding knowledge train to develop coding skills in three different learning pathways: Data Science, Mobile App Development and Web Development through the provision of adequate resources, guidance, and mentorship. We use free learning resources from FreeCodeCamp and the financial aid programme of Coursera.

Our Aim: Â· To assist unemployed youth, empower women to develop employability skills and build a solid foundation in a chosen career path in IT.

Our Objectives:

  • To reduce the jobless rate by giving opportunities to highly dedicated individuals to gain digital skills that they can leverage for employment opportunities.
  • To assist individuals who cannot afford expensive Bootcamps by providing an enabling environment for them to develop their employability skills to achieve their IT dreams.
