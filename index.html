2022 Has been an amazing yesr for us at Littledrop Education Bootcamp

Little Drop Education Coding Bootcamp is an online-based not-for-profit charity where our mentees with little or no coding knowledge train to develop coding skills in three different learning pathways: Data Science, Mobile App Development and Web Development through the provision of adequate resources, guidance, and mentorship. We use free learning resources from https://www.freecodecamp.org/ and the financial aid programme of Coursera. Aim: · To assist unemployed youth, empower women to develop employability skills and build a solid foundation in a chosen career path in IT. Objectives: · To reduce the jobless rate by giving opportunities to highly dedicated individuals to gain digital skills that they can leverage for employment opportunities. · To assist individuals who cannot afford expensive Bootcamps by providing an enabling environment for them to develop their employability skills to achieve their IT dreams.
